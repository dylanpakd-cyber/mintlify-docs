FAQ

## Before 
you _start._

What people ask before they subscribe. Have another? [Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/t4Dqu5r9zh).

I have a brand-new account. Will it get banned if I start posting?+

That's the riskiest moment, so Warmr won't let a fresh account post on day one. It warms the account up first, scrolling, liking, following, and searching like a real user through a phased ramp over days, until it's matured and ready. Only then does it join your posting rotation, and from there Warmr keeps it warm daily so it stays healthy. Works on TikTok and Instagram.

Will my accounts stay healthy over time?+

No tool can promise outcomes, and anyone who does is lying. Here's what Warmr controls: it drives real iPhones, taps like a human, and warms every account (scroll, like, follow, search) before it posts. That's what keeps accounts healthy for months. You control a clean IP per account and a sane posting cadence.

What happens when an app changes its layout?+

Warmr reads each platform from real screenshots, and we ship a patch within 24 to 48 hours when a layout shifts. Your app pulls it on the next check-in. You rebuild nothing.

What if a device hangs mid-upload?+

Warmr catches the stall, restarts the iPhone-side process, and resumes from the same account. Nothing double-posts, because state is checkpointed to disk. Even if your Mac reboots, the run picks up from the last checkpoint.

Do I need proxies? Will accounts get banned?+

Bans come from network identity as much as behavior, and many accounts on one home IP is the fastest way to get flagged. Warmr paces the on-device behavior; you give each account its own mobile or residential IP, a 4G proxy, or its own SIM. We show you the exact setup on the call.

Where does my data live?+

On the Mac you own, on your own network: accounts, devices, and the activity log all stay local. Cloud Drop just queues your uploads; once your Mac pulls a clip, your credentials and source files never leave home.

Who is this for?+

Solo founders, indie operators, and one-person growth teams posting to TikTok and Instagram at scale, with X and LinkedIn kept warm. The builder who'd rather plug in three iPhones than hire three VAs. Start with one, add more when you're ready.

What does Warmr not do?+

It doesn't buy or sell accounts, scrape inboxes, touch unofficial APIs, or break platform rules. It paces, runs, and logs every action on real devices you own.

How do I get access?+
