Warmr offers four plans built around the number of iPhones you run and accounts you manage. Every plan includes shadowban-safe warmups, auto-posting on a schedule, and support for TikTok, Instagram, X, and LinkedIn, so you pick the tier that matches your device count and storage needs, then scale up when you're ready.

## Plan comparison

| | Free | Solo | Rack | Scale |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $0 | $40/mo | $80/mo | $150/mo |
| **iPhones** | 1 | 1 | 3 | Unlimited |
| **Accounts** | 8 | 8 | 24 | Unlimited |
| **Cloud Drop** | 500 MB | 5 GB | 20 GB | Unlimited |
| **Carousels + music** | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Warmup** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Auto-posting** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

## Billing and payments

Warmr's billing is handled by [Lemon Squeezy](https://lemonsqueezy.com), the Merchant of Record for all purchases. Lemon Squeezy processes your payment and handles applicable taxes, so you don't need to manage VAT or sales tax separately.

Once you complete checkout, your license key is emailed to you immediately and is also visible in your dashboard at [app.warmr.so](https://app.warmr.so). For details on activating your key, see the [License Key guide](/billing/license-key).
