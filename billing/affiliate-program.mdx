## How attribution works

Attribution is **last-referrer** (last click). When someone clicks your link, Warmr and LemonSqueezy store a referral identifier for **30 days**. If they subscribe within that window, the commission is credited to you. If they later click a different affiliate's link before subscribing, the most recent link wins.

Because commissions are recurring, you keep earning 25% on each renewal for as long as that customer stays subscribed — the 30-day window only governs the *initial* attribution, not how long you get paid.

<Note>
The 30-day window is matched end-to-end: the warmr.so site sets a 30-day affiliate cookie, and LemonSqueezy uses a 30-day referral window, so a referral never falls through the gap between a free trial and the paid conversion.
</Note>

## Payouts

LemonSqueezy handles all affiliate payouts:

- **Schedule:** the 1st and the 15th of each month.
- **Clearing:** a referral is paid once it has cleared its 30-day window (covering refunds and chargebacks).
- **Minimum:** $10 USD — balances below that roll over to the next payout.
- **Method:** PayPal or bank transfer, set in your affiliate hub.

<Warning>
Self-referrals don't qualify. Because affiliate commissions are recurring, buying through your own link to get a standing discount is not allowed — we approve affiliates manually and remove self-referred orders.
</Warning>
