Warmr is a Mac desktop app built for solo founders, indie operators, and one-person growth teams who want to run a professional social media operation without the overhead of a full team. Instead of spending hours on manual posting or risking account bans with bot-based tools, Warmr lets your Mac silently drive a rack of real iPhones: tapping, scrolling, and posting exactly the way a human would.

## The Problem Warmr Solves

Managing social accounts at scale is brutally time-consuming. Posting manually across TikTok, Instagram, X, and LinkedIn eats hours every week. Third-party automation tools that touch unofficial APIs get accounts flagged and banned. Renting a managed iPhone farm is expensive and hands you zero control. Warmr puts that control back in your hands: your iPhones, your Mac, your schedule. Fully automated.

## How It Works

Warmr connects to the physical iPhones sitting on your desk via USB. Before any account starts posting, Warmr runs it through a **warmup lifecycle**: scrolling feeds, liking content, following accounts, and running searches, so the account builds a realistic activity history that platforms trust. Once an account clears warmup, Warmr picks up your content from **Cloud Drop** (uploaded from your phone or laptop) and posts it on the schedule you define. One Mac can control 10 or more iPhones simultaneously, each running multiple accounts.

<Note>
  Warmr never touches unofficial APIs. Every action is performed via real on-screen taps on physical iPhones you own.
</Note>
