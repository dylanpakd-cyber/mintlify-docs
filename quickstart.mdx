---
title: "Get started with Warmr in 10 minutes"
sidebarTitle: "Quickstart"
description: "Sign up for Warmr, install the Mac app, connect your first iPhone, and run your first account warmup — all in under 10 minutes."
---

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from zero to a live account warmup — signing up, installing the Mac app, connecting an iPhone, and kicking off your first warmup session. By the end, Warmr will be running on your Mac and actively warming your first social account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for Warmr">
    Go to [https://app.warmr.so/signup](https://app.warmr.so/signup) and sign in with Google. Your license key is delivered instantly to your email and dashboard — no credit card required. The free 7-day trial gives you full access to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and install the Mac app">
    After signing up, open your Warmr dashboard and download the Mac app installer. See [Installation](/installation) for step-by-step help with the .dmg and macOS security prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your license key">
    Open Warmr from your Applications folder. When prompted, paste the license key from your signup email or dashboard and click **Activate**. Warmr will confirm activation and take you to the main interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect an iPhone">
    Plug your iPhone into your Mac via USB. Unlock the iPhone and tap **Trust** when it asks whether to trust this computer. Within a few seconds, the device appears in Warmr's device list, ready to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a social account">
    Select your connected iPhone in Warmr and choose **Add Account**. Pick the platform — TikTok, Instagram, X, or LinkedIn — and log in to the account directly on the iPhone through the Warmr interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start your first warmup">
    Select the account you just added and press **Start**. Warmr immediately begins the warmup phase — scrolling feeds, liking posts, and following accounts — to build a natural activity history before any scheduled posts go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can connect additional iPhones at any time. Each iPhone can run up to 8 accounts per platform.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Fresh accounts go through a warmup phase before they join your posting rotation. This usually takes a few days depending on the platform.
</Note>
